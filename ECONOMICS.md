# Economics of SpendWise

## Unit Economics
- **Cost Per Audit (CPA)**: 
  - Vercel Hosting: ~$0.001 (distributed)
  - Anthropic API: ~$0.02 per summary (Claude 3.5 Sonnet)
  - Resend Email: Free tier ($0)
  - **Total Variable Cost**: ~$0.021 per audit.
- **Customer Acquisition Cost (CAC)**:
  - Through organic GTM (HN, X, Word of mouth): **$0.00**.
  - Through paid (optional): Est. $5.00 per lead.
- **Lead Value (LTV of a lead)**:
  - Average AI spend of target startup: $1,500/mo.
  - Credex potential savings: 20% ($300/mo).
  - Credex margin (est. 10% of spend): $150/mo.
  - **Estimated Annual Lead Value**: $1,800.

## Conversion Math (The Funnel)
To be profitable and drive **$1M ARR**:
1.  **Audits**: 10,000 / month.
2.  **Lead Capture (20%)**: 2,000 / month.
3.  **Consultation Booked (5%)**: 100 / month.
4.  **Credit Purchase (30%)**: 30 / month.
5.  **Average Annual Deal**: $3,000 (Credex margin).
6.  **Monthly New Revenue**: $90,000.
7.  **Total ARR After 12 Months**: ~$1.08M.

## What has to be true for $1M ARR?
- **Retention**: Startups must stay on the AI tools for at least 18 months.
- **Conversion**: The "Audit -> Consultation" bridge must be seamless. This is why we prioritize the >$500 savings CTA.
- **Credit Inventory**: Credex must have sufficient inventory of the tools identified in the audits.
