# Go-To-Market (GTM) Strategy - SpendWise

## Target User
The exact target user for SpendWise is the **Engineering Manager (EM)** or **Head of Operations** at a Seed to Series B startup (10–100 employees). 
- **The Pain**: They are the ones approving the monthly credit card spend for Cursor, Claude, and OpenAI. They feel the "subscription creep" but don't have the time to manually audit every seat or negotiate enterprise pricing.
- **The Context**: They are likely scrolling through X (formerly Twitter) or Hacker News looking for ways to optimize their burn rate in a "default alive" era.

## Where They Hang Out
- **X (Twitter)**: Following accounts like @paulg, @garrytan, and AI-specific influencers like @karpathy.
- **Hacker News (Show HN)**: The primary launchpad for dev-focused tools.
- **Slack/Discord**: "Founder-led" communities like Y Combinator's Bookface or specific AI Engineering Discords.
- **Subreddits**: r/startups, r/artificial, r/SaaS.

## The First 100 Users ($0 Budget)
1.  **The "Show HN" Launch**: Post a clean, data-driven "Show HN: We built a 60-second audit for your AI spend." Focus on the "No Login Required" and "Private" aspects to reduce friction.
2.  **Targeted X Thread**: Create a thread: "We analyzed 50+ AI tool pricing pages so you don't have to. Here's how startups are wasting $4k/year on AI." Link to the tool.
3.  **Indie Hackers / Product Hunt**: Launch on a Tuesday (high traffic) with a focus on the "Savings" angle.
4.  **Direct DM to YC Founders**: Reach out to 20-30 current batch founders with a personalized message: "Hey, saw you're using Cursor/Claude. We built this free tool to check if you're on the optimal plan. No login, takes 1 min."

## The Unfair Distribution Channel
**Credex’s Existing Ecosystem**: Credex already has relationships with companies looking for discounted credits. By embedding SpendWise as the "first step" in the Credex onboarding, we create a natural funnel. Every company that wants to buy credits *first* gets an audit. If the audit finds $500+ in savings, the lead is already "warm" and mathematically convinced.

## Week 1 Traction Goals
- **500 Audits Completed**: High-intent users exploring the tool.
- **100 Leads Captured (20% conversion)**: Users who want the full report.
- **5 Credex Consultations Booked**: High-value leads looking for immediate credits.
- **10+ Shared Public URLs**: The "Viral Loop" in action.
